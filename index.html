Switzerland Milk Production

The interactive charts present data on milk production by canton in Switzerland between 2021 and 2023, highlighting the amount of milk sold in tons (Verkehrsmilch in Tonnen). The visualization provides a detailed comparison between the cantons and their contribution to the country's total milk production, with information provided by TSM Treuhand GmbH.
